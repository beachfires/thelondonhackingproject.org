Welcome to The London Hacking Project

We are a community-driven tech non-profit organization dedicated to making technology education accessible to everyone in London. Through regular meetups, workshops, and collaborative sessions, we provide a platform for learning and sharing knowledge about programming, information security, data science, and more.

Whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced professional, our inclusive environment welcomes all skill levels. Join us to learn new skills, work on exciting projects, and connect with like-minded individuals passionate about technology.
